Ingredients

  • 400 gr. spageti
  • 200 gr. chorizo
  • 250 ml. Tomato pure
  • 250 ml. Water

Procedure

  1. Put the chorizo in a recipient with 250 ml. of water
  2. When it starts to boil add the spageti and tomato pure
  3. Cook until spageti is aldente
